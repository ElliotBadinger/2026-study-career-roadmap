Part 4: The Financial and Logistical Plan

NSFAS Funding Deep Dive

Eligibility Criteria for 2026

  • • South African citizen or permanent resident
  • • Household income ≤ R350,000/year (R600,000 if disabled)
  • • First-time entering student at public institution
  • • Passed Grade 12 & meet admission requirements
  • • Enrolled in NSFAS-approved course

Source: [12]

Good News

Having Mathematical Literacy does NOT disqualify you from NSFAS funding. Eligibility is based on financial need and academic requirements, not specific matric subjects.

Other Funding Avenues

Funza Lushaka Bursary

For teaching careers - covers tuition, accommodation, and living allowance. Requires teaching at public schools after graduation.

Perfect match for Foundation Phase teaching career path.

University-Specific Bursaries

Most universities offer their own bursaries and grants based on academic and financial criteria. Check with each institution's financial aid office.

Learnerships & Apprenticeships

Work-based learning programs combining theory with practical experience, often fully funded by employers.
